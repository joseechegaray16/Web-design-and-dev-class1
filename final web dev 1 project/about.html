Current

I am a lifelong learner who takes pride in being in uncomfortable situations. I am self taught in every creative and artistic domain I happen to be dabbling in.

I enjoy being a jack of all trades as my projects will prove that to be the case. I don't have a one size fits all approach when dealing with new customers or any project that I decide to take on. I enjoy working with other people as I believe inspiration can strike at any moment and collaboration is an essential component to any project.



History

My journey to being a digital nomad wasn't a direct one. For the longest time my interests were leaning more towards something in the medical field rather than the technological sector. However, I found myself feeling depleted and suffering from severe loss of motivation. So I began exploring other areas of potential work that seemed interesting and sure enough I found what it was I wanted to do.
